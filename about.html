When asked why the word "industries" is in the company's name and not something like the word "blog," Mike had this to say: "Well, I'm just doing this for a class assignment and the example had the word industries in it so I just decided to use that. I then added my name to the company's name just to prove that I didn't copy and paste anything and that I actually typed this all out by hand. Now get out of my sight, I have an About Me to write right now!" Mike Kleinbriel then fired me and I'm now typing this About Me inside a bathroom in my Mom's basement.
